The Director of Educational Outreach provides leadership for the continuing education/professional development activities of the College of Education which extend the benefit of UNC Charlotte’s regular academic and research activities to practicing teachers, counselors, and school leaders. Specific responsibilities include the following:

  • Work with faculty to design and deliver lectures, seminars, and conferences for the continuing professional development of teachers, counselors, and school leaders. Provide management services for existing initiatives such as the Annual Child & Family Development Conference and the McLeod Institute for Substance Abuse Counselors.
  • Serve as the College’s liaison with the Southwest Education Alliance to plan and deliver collaborative professional development initiatives.
  • Work collaboratively with groups on and off campus that share a commitment to effective professional development for educators (e.g., Charlotte Chamber, Charlotte Advocates for Education, etc.).
